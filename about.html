ABOUT OUR FIRM

My name is Jamshidbek Ibrokhimov, and I am dedicated to helping individuals legally immigrate to the United States. With a passion for assisting immigrants and fluency in both English and Uzbek, I am confident in my ability to support you through the process of preparing your documents. I hold a Law Degree from Uzbekistan and have pursued a Master of Laws (LL.M.) at The Pennsylvania State University in the United States. My education and work experience have equipped me with strong skills in legal document drafting, research, and consultation. As a legal counsel at the Department of Justice of Tashkent Region, Uzbekistan I provided timely and accurate legal advice to state organizations and drafted legal documents to ensure their full legal rights. Currently, I am working as a Law Clerk at a U.S.-based immigration law firm in Pittsburgh, PA, where we assist many individuals in immigrating to the USA.
